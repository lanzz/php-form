Method Form->is_submitted

Parameters: none

Returns: boolean

Returns true if the form has been submitted, or false otherwise.

The form is considered submitted if it has been instantiated with at least one submitted value, i.e. from_array() with a non-empty array, or from_get(), from_post() or from_request() with any $_GET and/or $_POST parameters within the name prefix of the form.
